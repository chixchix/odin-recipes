Eggs and avocados

Description

My go to breakfast, I make this almost every day, the classic and timeless eggs and avocados. You literally can never go wrong with this. It is stress free, and life made easy for a light morning breakfast.

Ingredients

  • 3 eggs
  • 1 avocado
  • Salt
  • Black pepper
  • Butter/oil

Steps

  1. Break in three eggs into a small bowl and add a pinch of salt and black pepper to your battered eggs.
  2. Spray your cooking oil of choice or butter in pan.
  3. Pour in your eggs and mix until its fried.
  4. Put your eggs in your plate and serve with sliced avocados.
